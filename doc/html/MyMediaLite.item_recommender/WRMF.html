WRMF Class

Weighted matrix factorization method proposed by Hu et al. and Pan et al.: Y. Hu, Y. Koren, and C. Volinsky: Collaborative filtering for implicit feedback datasets. In IEEE International Conference on Data Mining (ICDM 2008), pages 263--272, 2008. R. Pan, Y.Zhou, B. Cao, N. N. Liu, R. M. Lukose, M. Scholz, and Q. Yang: One-class collaborative filtering. In IEEE International Conference on Data Mining (ICDM 2008), pages 502--511, 2008. We use the fast computation method proposed by Hu et al. and we allow a global weight to penalize observed/unobserved values. This engine does not support online updates.
